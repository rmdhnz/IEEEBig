Hands-On Training

The activity held by IEEE BIG is in the form of a workshop or training to introduce IoT and Arduino IDE in order to develop the soft and hard skills of the trainees. The program activities also aim to enhance, expand, and create an inclusive and broad network for members to meet and share viewpoints with members outside the industry. At the end of each session, participants will be given an evaluation task according to the material presented by the speaker.

About

  • - Increase participants' theoretical knowledge and insight in the field of Internet of Things.
  • -Increase participants' insight in the field of electronic technology and information technology, especially in the field of IoT

  • Introducing the concept of IoT and Ubiquitos Computing to participants
  • Explaining the application, benefits and potential of IoT in Indonesia.

this event will be held on a hybrid basis, via zoom platform and laboratory in Electrical Engineering Department
