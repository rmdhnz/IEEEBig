Competition

To help increase curiosity as well as to provide ideas or innovations for the use of IoT in the construction of new nation capital, IEEEBIG holds an essay competition with the theme “Utilizing IoT for Efficiency and Productivity In The New Nation’s Capital”.

In addition, IEEEBIG itself will contain many things, such as hands-on training, community service, essay competition, and company fair.

About

“Utilizing IoT for Efficiency and Productivity in The New Nation's Capital

  • Participants
    • The participants of the Essay Competition of IEEEBIG are people that are registered in the database, which is an active student registered at one of the Universities in Indonesia, can register individually or form a team consisting of 2 people, with 1 person as the chairman and the other as member. Participants can come from different study programs and class years but still in the same college.
  • Committee
    • The committee of the Essay Competition of IEEEBIG is a member of the IEEEBIG.

Date : 26th November - 18th December 2022
Media : Online
